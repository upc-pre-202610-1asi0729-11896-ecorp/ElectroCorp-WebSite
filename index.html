Start-Up Description

We are a tech startup focused on democratizing home automation and energy efficiency for Peruvian households.

Currently, making a house "smart" is expensive, requires complex work (cables, technicians) and the applications are usually generic or difficult to understand, in addition to the high cost of electricity.

A plug-and-play IoT ecosystem that installs in minutes on existing switches and sockets. It allows for remote control and real-time consumption monitoring without the need for any construction work in the home.



Our Goals

To develop an easy-to-understand plug-and-play Internet of Things (IoT) ecosystem that allows families and new businesses in Peru to efficiently manage their electrical consumption, optimizing their economic expenses and promoting sustainability through a software platform: an app and/or website.
